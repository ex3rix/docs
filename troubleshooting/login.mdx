## Unable to log in?

<Steps>
    <Step title="Check your credentials">
        Please ensure you are using the **correct** email & password when logging in!
    </Step>
    <Step title="Reset Your Password">
        Forgot your password? Don't worry! Click on `Forgot Password` on the [login page](https://haunt.gg/login) to reset it!
    </Step>
    <Step title="Discord Login Issues">
        If you log in with **Discord**, ensure you're using the **correct** Discord account!
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
    Still unable to log in? Open a support ticket in our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/hauntbio)!
</Warning>
